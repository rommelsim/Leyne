# Leyne — Support

**Leyne** shows you live Singapore bus arrival times and lets you pin the
stops you use, so the next bus is one tap away.

For help, questions, bug reports, or feedback, email
**commute668@gmail.com**. We aim to reply within a few business days.
Please include your OS version (iOS or Android) and device model, and a
screenshot if relevant.

## Frequently asked questions

**No buses are showing for a stop.**
Arrivals come live from LTA DataMall. Outside service hours, or if LTA has
no prediction for that service yet, a stop can legitimately show nothing.
Pull to refresh, check another stop, and confirm you have a network
connection.

**It can't find my location / no nearby stops.**
Leyne needs location access to show nearby stops:

- **iOS:** Settings ▸ Leyne ▸ Location ▸ While Using the App
- **Android:** Settings ▸ Apps ▸ Leyne ▸ Permissions ▸ Location ▸
  Allow only while using the app

You can still search stops by name or 5-digit code without location.

**How do I pin a stop?**
Open Nearby or Search, tap a stop, then tap **Pin to Home**. Pinned stops
appear on the Home tab with live arrivals. To reorder, use the drag handle
on the right of each card; to rename, tap the pencil icon next to the
stop label.

**How do I customise which buses appear on my Home card?**
Open a pinned stop (tap the card), then use the bookmark/check toggles
beside each service to add or remove it from your Home view. The card
shows up to three services at a glance with a "+N more" chip if there
are more.

**What does the green "ARRIVING" badge mean?**
Any service whose next bus is one minute or less away triggers the
arriving highlight on the card and row — a green hairline border, the
ARRIVING pill, and a green "Arr" ETA.

**Why do I see ads, and are they tracking me?**
Leyne is free and shows banner ads via Google AdMob. On first launch
you're asked for consent — a Google consent form where it's required
(e.g. the EEA/UK), and on iOS, Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.
If you **allow** tracking, ads can be personalized using your device's
advertising identifier; if you **decline**, you still see ads, but
non-personalized ones. You can change this anytime:

- **iOS:** Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Tracking
- **Android:** Settings ▸ Security & privacy ▸ More privacy & security ▸ Ads

See the privacy policy for full details.

**Will there be a Home Screen widget or Lock Screen live countdown?**
These were in the iOS-native v1.0 of Leyne and are planned to come back to
the cross-platform version after the Android launch. The new build uses a
single codebase so both iOS and Android stay in sync; bringing back
iOS-only features (Live Activity, Widget) is a deliberate later phase.

## Privacy

Leyne stores your pins and settings only on your device and has no user
accounts. Full details: see the Privacy Policy linked alongside this page.
