# Leyne — Support

**Leyne** shows you live Singapore bus arrival times, lets you pin the stops
you use, and can put the next bus on your Lock Screen and Home Screen.

For help, questions, bug reports, or feedback, email
**commute668@gmail.com**. We aim to reply within a few business days. Please
include your iOS version and device model, and a screenshot if relevant.

## Frequently asked questions

**No buses are showing for a stop.**
Arrivals come live from LTA DataMall. Outside service hours, or if LTA has no
prediction for that service yet, a stop can legitimately show nothing. Pull to
refresh, check another stop, and confirm you have a network connection.

**It can't find my location / no nearby stops.**
Leyne needs location access to show nearby stops. Enable it in
**iOS Settings ▸ Leyne ▸ Location ▸ While Using the App**. You can still
search stops by name or code without location.

**How do I pin a stop?**
Open a stop from Nearby or Search and tap **Pin to Home** (or the pin button).
Pinned stops appear on the Home tab with live arrivals. Press and hold a
pinned card to reorder.

**How do I use the Live Activity?**
Open a bus and tap **Start Live Activity**. The next-arrival countdown appears
on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island. Open the same bus again and tap
**Stop Live Activity** to end it.

**How do I add the Home Screen widget?**
Long-press your Home Screen ▸ **+** ▸ search **Leyne** ▸ add the **Pinned
Stop** widget. Long-press the widget ▸ **Edit Widget** to choose which pinned
stop it shows. Pin a stop in the app first so it can be selected.

**Why do I see ads, and are they tracking me?**
Leyne is free and shows banner ads via Google AdMob. The app does not run the
tracking-permission prompt and serves ads in a limited mode. See the privacy
policy for details.

## Privacy

Leyne stores your pins and settings only on your device and has no user
accounts. Full details: see the Privacy Policy linked alongside this page.
